Rules

Two player play this game in 3x3 board.

Player complete 3-in-the-row with same parity or complete a row with sum = 15 win the game.

First player can place [1,3,5,7,9]; Second player can place [2,4,6,8].



How to play

Select a number from left number pad and click the blank of the board.

Press "AI Move" to let AI take its action.

Press "Restart" to restart the game.



Reminder: You may wait for completion of AI operation for few seconds.

Press the X button on the right corner to close this guide.
